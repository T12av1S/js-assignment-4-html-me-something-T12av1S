My Guitar Heroes

by Travis J.

Those that shaped me into the guitarist I am today.

Synyster Gates

Brian Elwin Haner, Jr., known professionally as Synyster Gates, is the lead guitarist of heavy metal band Avenged Sevenfold.
Following in his father's footsteps, Synyster gravitated to the guitar at a very young age. Due to this, he has had a hand in multiple musical genres over the years, such as gypsy jazz, hard rock and metal. He was ranked amongst the Top 50 fastest guitar players of all-time by Guitar World Magazine

B.B. King

Riley B. King, known professionally as B.B. King, was an American guitarist and singer that helped shaped the sound of the Blues genre as it is known today. With musical hits such as "Rock Me Baby", "The Thrill is Gone", and "Everyday I Have the Blues", amongst other artistic contributions, B.B. rightfully earned the title of
"The King of Blues".

John Mayer

John Clayton Mayer, is an American singer, songwriter and guitarist. He has released hits such as "Slow Dancing in a Burning Room", "Waiting on the World to Change", and "Gravity" to critical acclaim. His guitar style is reminiscent of other guitarists such as Jimi Hendrix, Stevie Ray Vaughan, and countless others. He has even collaborated with another guitarist on this list; B.B. King himself!
